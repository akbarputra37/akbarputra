BYD EV Website





Introduction

BYD, which stands for “Build Your Dreams”, is an electric car manufacturer from Shenzhen, China. BYD is currently the best and best-selling electric car manufacturer worldwide. BYD, which is supported by influential investors such as Warren Buffet, has now beaten Tesla on the sales side and beaten on the battery technology side. BYD has advantages in battery technology. BYD started as the world’s largest manufacturer of rechargeable batteries, so now they can produce electric cars that can travel longer distances and are more efficient than other competitors. BYD also successfully sold 314,760 units from the fourth quarter of 2023 to the first quarter of 2024, followed by Tesla which sold 140,683 units. Because BYD is currently the largest and best electric car manufacturer in the world, BYD needs improvements and improvements to its website to provide a better experience for users and also to support service improvements and increase good impressions for users and potential users.

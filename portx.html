PortX Mobile App





Introduction

PortX is a startup platform and application for buying and selling, trading, and also saving your crypto and stocks. At PortX you can easily buy and sell crypto and or your stock shares easily. You can trade on the spot market or perpetual market easily. And you can also save your crypto assets and stock shares at PortX. The PortX startup haves headquartered in Dubai UAE and has a target market focus in the West Asia and Eastern Europe regions such as Russia and Ukraine and surrounding areas. We design the PortX mobile application and also the PortX website.
